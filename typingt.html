Typing Test

Rules

  • Click on the input field to type.
  • The moment you start typing ,the timer will begin.
  • Orange characters denote the letters to be typed.
  • Red characters denote the letters typed incorrectly.
  • Green characters denote the letters typed correctly.
  • After completing the sentence,press any alphabet key to stop the timer and see the results.

  • WPM - Words per minute
